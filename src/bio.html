I knew it when the world was going digital and therefore I developed my skills in marketing and got into the digital aspect of it but what encouraged me the most to make the switch were several elements. One of those elements was my experience in switching careers. In 2007 I made a huge leap from successful career in food to media/advertising/marketing. I literally went from being a senior employee and started over as a junior. I found it very successful and fruitful as I learned a lot from it, although I started as a junior, but it didn’t take me much to climb up the ladder as I already had a decade of experience under my sleeve. My “can do” mentality is one of my strongest USPs. Suddenly getting involved in the web development which I was thinking of became no longer enough! I started itching to dig deeper.
